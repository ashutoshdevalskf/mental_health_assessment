About Our Platform

Welcome to Mind +, a self-assessment platform designed to help you better understand your mental health. Our goal is to make evidence-based screening tools accessible, private, and easy to use from anywhere. We combine research-backed questionnaires with a clean interface to give you insight into your current mental wellbeing.

Our Assessments

All assessments on this platform are derived from clinically validated tools used in mental health practice and research. Your responses are completely private and are meant to offer guidance — not a diagnosis.

General Mental Health Test

This composite screening includes questions from PHQ‑9, GAD‑7, and OCI‑R. It's designed to offer a broad overview of your mental state.

Based on: PHQ‑9, GAD‑7, OCI‑R Take Test

PHQ‑9 Depression Assessment

A widely-used tool to measure the severity of depression symptoms. Useful for self-monitoring over time.

Spitzer et al., 2001 Take Test

GAD‑7 Anxiety Assessment

GAD‑7 is a 7-item scale that screens for and measures the severity of generalized anxiety disorder.

Spitzer et al., 2006 Take Test

OCI‑R OCD Assessment

The Obsessive-Compulsive Inventory‑Revised is used to assess and monitor OCD symptoms and their severity.

Foa et al., 2002 Take Test
